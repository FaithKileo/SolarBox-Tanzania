About SolarBox Tanzania

SolarBox Tanzania Ltd. is a technology-driven startup founded in 2025, headquartered in Dar es Salaam. Born from a university research project at the University of Dar es Salaam, our mission is to modernize last-mile delivery in Tanzania's urban centers through innovative, sustainable solutions.

We specialize in designing and manufacturing solar-powered public delivery safe boxes that integrate advanced security features, renewable energy, and flexible access technology. Our team comprises engineers, technologists, and logistics experts dedicated to enhancing delivery efficiency, reducing environmental impact, and improving customer convenience.

Our Concept

SolarBox Tanzania aims to transform urban package delivery in Tanzania by deploying solar-powered, secure safe box systems that offer flexible pick-up capabilities. This innovative solution addresses the challenges of failed deliveries, recipient unavailability, and infrastructure limitations in Tanzania's major cities, leveraging renewable energy and hybrid online-offline technology for reliable, sustainable, and cost-effective last-mile logistics.

Manufacturing

Designing and producing solar-powered public delivery safe boxes equipped with integrated power, control, and security systems.

Service

Providing installation, maintenance, and operational support for the safe box network, along with a web-based authentication and notification platform for users.

What Makes Us Unique

The novelty lies in the integration of:

  • Solar Power Technology: Ensures uninterrupted operation in areas with unreliable grid electricity, a common challenge in Tanzania, while promoting sustainability.
  • Hybrid Online-Offline Functionality: Operates seamlessly with or without internet connectivity, overcoming Tanzania's inconsistent network coverage.
  • Secure, Flexible Delivery Solution: Dual-password authentication and 24/7 access reduce failed deliveries and enhance security, tailored to urban Tanzania's unique logistical and infrastructural challenges.
  • Cost and Environmental Efficiency: Reduces delivery costs by 30-40% and carbon emissions by 25-30%, offering a scalable model for developing regions.

Unlike existing systems like DHL Pack stations or Japan's PUDO points, SolarBox is specifically designed for developing urban environments with limited infrastructure, making it a pioneering solution for Tanzania and potentially other emerging markets.
